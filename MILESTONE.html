About Our Project & Team

Team Name: CRIMSON CREW

Problem Statement: AI & IoT Powered Intelligent System for Real-Time Elephant Movement Detection & Prevention of Human-Elephant Conflict (HEC)

Project Summary:

  • IoT-based smart surveillance and alert system deployed at villages and forest borders using multimodal sensors (thermal/IR cameras, PIR, acoustic, vibration, drones).
  • AI/ML algorithms for vision and audio classification optimized for edge devices provide real-time alerts via mobile app, SMS, loudspeakers, and sirens.
  • Use of non-harmful deterrents like lights, ultrasonic sound, chili-smoke, and drones to prevent conflicts.
  • Cloud dashboard for hotspot detection, movement prediction, and analytics assists forest officials with monitoring and decision-making.

Innovation & Impact:

  • Multimodal fusion (vision + audio + vibration) reduces false positives for reliable detection.
  • Edge AI inference enables real-time detection even without internet connectivity.
  • Solar-powered, low-cost, durable hardware designed for remote rural deployment.
  • Supports wildlife conservation, prevents property loss, and aligns with environmental sustainability goals.

About Smart India Hackathon: The Smart India Hackathon is a nationwide initiative that empowers students to solve real-world problems leveraging technology and innovation. Achieving milestones within this competition demonstrates dedication, skill enhancement, and teamwork.
